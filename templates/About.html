About MindLobby

Revolutionizing collaborative learning through technology

Our Mission

MindLobby was created with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform the way students study and learn together. In an increasingly digital world, we recognized the need for a platform that combines the engagement of multiplayer gaming with the effectiveness of modern study techniques.

Our platform leverages AI-powered document processing to create interactive learning experiences, making study sessions more engaging, collaborative, and effective. Whether you're preparing for exams, reviewing course materials, or learning with friends, MindLobby brings minds together in a shared pursuit of knowledge.

Collaborative Learning

Foster teamwork and peer-to-peer learning through real-time multiplayer study sessions

AI-Powered

Automatically generate quizzes and flashcards from your study materials using advanced AI

Gamified Experience

Make learning fun with competitive elements that keep students engaged and motivated

About This Project

Academic Thesis

MindLobby is a thesis project developed as part of our academic program, focusing on the intersection of education technology, collaborative learning, and artificial intelligence.

Technology Stack

Built with Flask, Socket.IO for real-time communication, PostgreSQL for data persistence, and modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

Future Goals

We aim to expand MindLobby with more game modes, enhanced AI capabilities, mobile applications, and integration with popular learning management systems.
